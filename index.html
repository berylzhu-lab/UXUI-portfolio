About me

I'm Creative UI/UX Designer from Seattle, Washington, studying at the University of Washington merging Digital Media, Engineering and Architecture to create intuitive, measurable experiences. I possess strong technical fluency in Figma, Unity, and Wordpress, allowing me to prototype rapidly and analyze results rigorously.

My job is to build your website so that it is functional and user-friendly but at the same time attractive. Moreover, I add personal touch to your product and make sure that is eye-catching and easy to use. My aim is to bring across your message and identity in the most creative way.
